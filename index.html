What is Chef's Dungeon?

This company was created by our owner/founder "Chef" in 2024 when he started a Professional ttrpg service. The business was running Dungeon & Dragons 5e campaigns and one-shots on a website called startplaying.games, where he would advertise these games. With growing popularity, he expanded the business to Chef's Dungeon Ttrpg. We offer a lot more than just Dungeons & Dragons with the expansion. Chef wanted to create a community where people could not only play Dungeons & Dragons, he wanted a community of people who play TTRPGs, MTG and even our friends in the video gaming community. We have a great staff of game masters and moderators on our server that all have the same goal of creating a great discord community. We also offer competitions & giveaways to our members.
